Our Story

A Hidden Gem on the Red Sea

Nestled between the dramatic Sinai mountains and the crystal-clear Red Sea in Nuweiba, Leley Camp is a place where time slows down. Our wooden cabins sit right on the water's edge, each with its own direct sea view.

Whether you come for the world-class coral reefs, the sunrise yoga sessions, or simply to disconnect and breathe — you'll leave as family.

Things To Do

Activities & Experiences

Every day at Leley Camp is a new adventure.

🤿

Snorkeling & Reefs

One of the richest coral reefs in the Red Sea is literally at your doorstep. Spot colourful fish in crystal-clear water.

🧘

Yoga by the Sea

Morning sessions in our open-air hall overlooking the water. All levels welcome — sunrise yoga is unforgettable.

☕

Bedouin Beach Lounge

Cushions on the sand, Bedouin carpets, mint tea, and a sea view. The best way to do absolutely nothing.
